## CURRENT GST RATE
GST is currently 9% in Singapore (effective 1 January 2024).
If the receipt shows a different rate, use the rate shown on the receipt.

## PRICE INTERPRETATION
- Receipts in Singapore show GROSS prices (GST-inclusive) on line items.
- The printed item price (e.g. 29.00) is the GROSS amount including GST.
- NET amount = GROSS / 1.09  (round to 2 decimal places)
- GST amount = GROSS - NET   (round to 2 decimal places)
- Example: Item $29.00 gross → Net $26.61 → GST $2.39

## TOTAL AND SUBTOTAL RULES
- "subtotal" must reflect the NET amount (before GST) of all paid items.
- "tax_amount" must reflect the total GST paid.
- "total" must reflect the final GROSS amount actually paid (cash tendered).
- subtotal + tax_amount = total  (verify this always holds true).
- If the receipt explicitly prints a "Net Amount" or "Before GST" line,
  use that value directly as subtotal.
- If the receipt explicitly prints a "GST" line, use that value directly
  as tax_amount.

## DISCOUNTS
- Discounts reduce the GROSS price before GST is calculated.
- GST is NOT applicable on the discount amount itself.
- If a discount is shown:
    discounted_gross = original_price - discount_amount
    net              = discounted_gross / 1.09
    gst              = discounted_gross - net
- Record "discount" as the face value of the discount shown on receipt.
- Do NOT apply GST to the discount field.

## FREE ITEMS
- Any item marked as FREE, $0.00, or with a negative line
  cancelling a previous line must NOT contribute to subtotal or total.
- Still include free items in line_items with unit_price: 0.00
  so the record is complete for audit purposes.

## NEGATIVE LINE ITEMS (REVERSALS / VOIDS)
- Some receipts show a negative amount (e.g. -29.00) to cancel
  a previously charged item.
- Include these in line_items with a negative unit_price.
- Subtract their value from the subtotal accordingly.
- Do NOT treat payment processor settlement lines (e.g. "ADYEN Visa -29.00")
  as product line items — these are payment records, not purchases.

## PAYMENT METHOD
- Extract the specific card network if visible:
  Visa, Mastercard, Amex, PayNow, GrabPay, NETS, Cash.
- Do NOT return generic "Card" if the actual network is printed on receipt.
- If card last 4 digits are printed, record them in the notes field.
  Example notes: "Visa ending 7067"

## SUPPLIER NAME
- Use the store name printed at the top of the receipt.
- Include the mall or location if printed.
  Example: "BHG Tampines Mall" not just "BHG".

## LINE ITEM DESCRIPTION
- Include the SKU or item code if printed before the description.
  Example: keep "1183536 S/BF BELT 29" — do NOT strip the leading number.
- The leading number is a product SKU, not a quantity.

## CURRENCY
- Default to SGD for all Singapore receipts unless another
  currency is explicitly printed on the receipt.

## GST REGISTRATION NUMBER
- If the receipt shows a GST Reg No (e.g. M2-0119092-4),
  record it in the notes field.
  Example notes: "GST Reg: M2-0119092-4 | Visa ending 7067"

## WHAT TO IGNORE
- Ignore loyalty point lines, member tier lines, and survey URLs.
- Ignore ADYEN, Nets, or payment gateway settlement lines.
- Ignore "Thank you" and promotional footer text.
- Ignore staff ID, terminal ID, and register number lines
  unless specifically needed for the reference field.
